Captain America Keychain Project

Objective:
To design and fabricate a Captain America-themed laser-cut keychain with enhanced aesthetics and structural precision.

Innovation:
Integrating layered acrylic/wood design with optional LED enhancement, ensuring durability, precision cutting, and strong visual impact.

Conclusion:
The project successfully combines creative design with practical fabrication techniques, resulting in a visually striking and functional keychain.
