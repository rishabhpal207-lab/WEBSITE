Captain America Keychain Prototype

This is the image prototype of our project that you are seeing on the keychain.

This project involves designing and fabricating a Captain America–themed keychain that combines aesthetics, mechanical innovation, and digital interactivity. An NFC tag is embedded on the back, programmed to link directly to a functional website created for this project.

The CO₂ laser–cut acrylic structure ensures precision and durability. The integrated NFC tag adds modern functionality, making this a blend of engineering, design, and smart technology.
